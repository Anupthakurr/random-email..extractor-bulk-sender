How to Get a Google App Password

Google no longer allows automated tools to sign in using your regular email password. Instead, you must generate a special 16-digit "App Password" to securely send bulk emails.

Step 1: Turn on 2-Step Verification

You cannot create an App Password unless 2-Step Verification is enabled on your Google account.

  1. Go to your Google Account Security settings.
  2. Scroll down to "How you sign in to Google".
  3. Ensure 2-Step Verification is turned ON.

Step 2: Generate the App Password

  1. Go directly to the App Passwords page.
  2. Google will ask you for a name. Type something like "Bulk Mailer App" and click Create.
  3. Google will display a yellow box containing a 16-letter passcode.
  4. Copy that passcode exactly. Do not include any spaces.

Step 3: Enter it in the Dashboard

Go back to the Bulk Mailer dashboard. In the "Sender Accounts" box, paste your email, a colon, and the 16-digit password like this:

your.email@gmail.com:abcdefghijklmnop
